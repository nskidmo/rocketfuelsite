What is Rocket Fuel?

Rocket Fuel @ Sam's Club is a high-impact development experience for high-potential Managers and Senior Managers within the Marketing Practice.

The program serves as both a recognition and acceleration mechanism for Marketing team members — helping to build the next generation of leaders who drive member-led growth, collaboration, and creativity across the organization.

"Your nomination reflects confidence in your leadership potential and your ability to shape the future of Sam's Club Marketing. Rocket Fuel is a signal of investment, readiness, and potential."

The program is designed to accelerate leadership readiness, deepen enterprise understanding, and strengthen cross-functional trust — all in direct alignment with ambitions set-out in our Marketing Evolution, Sam's Club values, and feedback from AES.

Career Growth

Clearer career growth and development pathways for high-potential leaders.

Cross-Team Connection

Stronger cross-team connection and collaboration across the organization.

Trust & Safety

Build trust, psychological safety, and recognition within teams.

Program Format

Our Spring 2026 Rocket Fuel @ Sam's Club program will be held over a three-day in-person workshop, focusing on 12 leadership themes.

In preparation, participants will read, watch, and listen to "inspiration" relevant to the program's themes, and complete short reflections.

Participants will be assigned to self-organizing squads, and members are encouraged to meet to discuss themes, answer questions, and support one another's growth prior to the program.

Squads will also work together during the program and following the program for a capstone project.

Capstone Project

Following the in-person workshop, in your squads, brainstorm 3-5 topics that you believe are either the biggest opportunities or challenges for Marketing at Sam's Club.

Pick the one topic that, if addressed or supported, would have the biggest positive impact to your team and culture, your ability to deliver value for members, your partner relationships, the quality of your work, and the business results you drive.

Develop a ~5-minute pitch using Rocket Fuel leadership themes that:

1

Define & Quantify

Clearly defines and quantifies the opportunity or challenge.

2

Outline Your Plan

Outlines your plan to address the opportunity and challenge.

3

Resources Needed

Outlines the realistic resources needed to implement your plan.

🦈

Shark Tank-Style Pitch

In April or May, you'll pitch Marketing SteerCo, Shark Tank-style, where Practice Leads are the Sharks, who can decide whether to sponsor your idea — and if multiple Practice Leads opt to sponsor, your squad picks the one you want!
